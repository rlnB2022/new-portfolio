About Me

In 2010, I delved into the world of coding by developing and selling Android apps on the Google Play Store, leading me to create a website landing page for a job placement agency and working for a start-up.

My main focus these days is building features, resolving tickets and expanding my knowledge by learning new tech (recently, looking deeper into Ruby on Rails!) In my free time, I've built many fun projects from the crazy ideas that are going on in my head.

With my free time, I'm either coaching my son's baseball team, hanging out with my wife, son and dog, or trying to get another victory royale with my friends.
